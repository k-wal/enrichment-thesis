27784172|t|Tzumin A and B, two new lignan derivatives from the barks of Sassafras tzumu
27784172|a|Two new lignan compounds, 5'-allyl-2,2'-dihydroxy-[1,1'-biphenyl]-5-carboxylic acid (1) and 4,4'-diallyl-[1,1'-biphenyl]-2,2'-diol (2), together with four known compounds (3-6), were isolated from the barks of Sassafras tzumu. The new compounds were determined by NMR ((1)H and (13)C NMR, HSQC, HMBC, (1)H-(1)H COSY, NOESY / ROESY), and MS analysis. Compounds 1-3 showed potent AChE inhibitory activities, with IC50 values of 2.00, 1.81 and 1.91 Î¼M, respectively.
